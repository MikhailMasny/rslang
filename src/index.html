Welcome!
This application was created for you to learn new english words with the help of various interesting games.
Main
English Learning
Learning new words, their pronunciation, meanings, uses. Work with the dictionary. Adding new words to vocabulary groups for better memorization.
Additional
Find a pair
The main idea of this application is to try to find cards consisting of a word and its meaning. At the end of the game, the final result will be displayed.
Additional
Savana
The main idea of this application is to develop your vocabulary. The more words you know, the more experience points you get. At the end of the game, the final result will be displayed.
Additional
Audio call
The main idea of this application is to listen and indicate the correct word from the proposed ones. At the end of the game, the final result will be displayed.
Additional
Sprint
The main idea of this application is to select true or false with the displayed pair the word - translation. At the end of the game, the final result will be displayed.
Additional
SpeakIt
The main idea of this application is to сlick on the words to hear their sound and after that click on the button and pronounce the words into the microphone. At the end of the game, the final result will be displayed.
Additional
English puzzle
The main idea of this application is to click on words, collect phrases. Words can be drag and drop. Select tooltips in the menu. At the end of the game, the final result will be displayed.
